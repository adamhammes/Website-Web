About me

My name is Pierre-Olivier Parisé. I'm currently a Ph. D. student at Laval University (QC, Ca) under the supervision of Thomas J. Ransford. I am currently an owner of a Alexander-Graham Bell Graduate scholarship and recently granted a 2 years-long graduate scholarship from FRQNT.

I got my master's degree in 2017 from Université du Québec à Trois-Rivières under the supervision of Dominic Rochon. I worked on multicomplex dynamical systems and studied the Mandelbrot sets in the setting of the multicomplex numbers.

You may contact me by email

Research Interests

My current research interests are problems related to approximations by polynomials in various spaces of holomorphic functions. In the past, I have been interested in the theory of dynamical systems. In particular, I have studied the Mandelbrot sets and the Julia sets in the framework of Multicomplex numbers.
